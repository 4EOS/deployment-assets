Update Your Outlook Signature

1 Download the Template

Click on the SignatureTemplate.html file attached to the email to download it to your computer.

2 Copy the Template

  1. Open the downloaded SignatureTemplate.html file (it will open in your browser)
  2. Select all content by pressing Ctrl+A (or Cmd+A on Mac)
  3. Copy it by pressing Ctrl+C (or Cmd+C on Mac)

3 Open Outlook Settings

  1. Go to outlook.office.com
  2. Click the Settings gear icon in the top right
  3. Navigate to Account → Signatures → New Signature

4 Create Your Signature

  1. Name your signature (e.g., "Medical Ventures")
  2. Click in the signature editor box
  3. Paste the signature by pressing Ctrl+V (or Cmd+V on Mac)
  4. Update your name and email
Important: Type your name before deleting the placeholder name to preserve the text formatting.

Click Save when finished.

5 Set as Default

Under "Select default signatures":

  • • For New Messages: Select Medical Ventures
  • • For Replies/Forwards: Select Medical Ventures

Click the blue Save button at the bottom.

✓ All done! Your signature will now appear on all emails.
